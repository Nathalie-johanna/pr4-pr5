About Us

Hi! Welcome to my page! My name is Nathalie and I am 24 years old. I have been riding since I was eight years old and horses have become my life passion. Right now I am training to enter competition. Next to that, our family breeds horses at home. On this page you will find stories about the horses I train and our progress. Feel free to tag along on our journey.

 

Horses

Binky

Binky is one of the horses that I lease part-time. This means that I help take care of him, while his owner spends time with her family. I help train him in dressage.

Pablo

Pablo is our one year old foal, from our stable 'De Volharding'. His mother is Wendy, a New-Forest mare. my goal is to break pablo into the saddle and to train him as a dressage horse.

Amaretto

Next to Binky and Pablo I also train at a barn. Amaretto is one of the horses I often train with and ride dressage tests with. Originally he is a showjumper, but together we train dressage.
